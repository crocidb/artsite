## About

**stuffy knows** is my first music project. It's basically guitar, bass, and synth work. Influenced by psychedelic rock, it takes some old riffs I had worked on over the years and consolidates into a cohesive sound.

I composed, arranged, recorded and produced all in my home office in Düsseldorf.
